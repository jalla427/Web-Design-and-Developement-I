Who We Are

Video Game Enthusiasts is a social gathering for all varieties of gamers. If you want to talk and play with fellow video game enthusiasts, this is the place for you. Feel free to bring consoles, computers, handheld devices, or just yourself. The club does not currently provide consoles but it's expected of members using our few provided televisions to play multiplayer games and welcome any who wish to join in the fun.


Where To Find Us

Video Game Enthusiasts meet in room 374 on Tuesdays and room 235 on Thursdays each week in the DUC. In the event of a short notice room change, members of our Discord will be notified and a sign will be left on our normal room door to redirect those not in Discord.
